Questions & Answers
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free plan is free forever with no credit card required. You get 1 journal, unlimited trade logs, basic analytics, and a limited calendar view — no strings attached.
Can I cancel my Pro plan?
Yes. You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime from the Stripe billing portal. After cancellation, you keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period, then revert to the Free plan. Your trades and data are never deleted.
What happens to my extra journals if I cancel?
If you downgrade from Pro to Free, your extra journals become read-only. All your data is preserved — you just can't add new trades to those journals until you re-upgrade. Your primary journal continues to work normally.
Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored securely in our database powered by Supabase. Row-level security ensures only you can access your journals and trades. Your data belongs to you — export it anytime with a Pro plan.
What analytics does Pro unlock?
Pro gives you the full analytics suite: hour-of-day heatmap (discover your best trading times), day-of-week performance, mood vs. PNL correlation, confidence tracking, timeframe breakdown, and much more. These insights help you identify and sharpen your trading edge.
Can I backup and transfer my trade data?
Yes — Pro users can export their entire journal as a .json backup (including images) or a .csv file for Excel/Google Sheets. You can also import a backup to restore all your trades, settings, tags, and preferences.
